### What is Hacktoberfest?
Hacktoberfest is an annual celebration of open-source, brought to you by DigitalOcean, Intel, and GitHub. From October 1st to October 31st, developers of all skill levels are encouraged to contribute to open-source projects. By submitting 4 quality pull requests, you can earn a limited-edition Hacktoberfest t-shirt or plant a tree in support of sustainability! ðŸŒ³
